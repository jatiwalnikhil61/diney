How it works

Four steps from scan
to satisfied customer

No training manuals. No printouts. No missed tickets. Just a clean workflow your team picks up in minutes.

01

Customer scans QR

Each table has a unique QR code. Customers scan, browse the menu, and place their order — no app download required.

02

Kitchen gets the ticket

Orders appear instantly on the Kitchen Dashboard. Chefs see live tickets with table numbers and item counts — no verbal relay needed.

03

Waiter is notified

When a dish is marked ready, the waiter's screen pings immediately. No shouting, no cold plates waiting under a lamp.

04

Owner tracks everything

Sales analytics, order history, and real-time table status — all in one owner dashboard. Know your peak hours before you feel them.
